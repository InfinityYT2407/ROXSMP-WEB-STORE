SERVER NETWORK RULES

Ensuring a fair, respectful, and competitive gaming environment for all RoxSmp players.

01. General Conduct & Chat Rules expand_more

We expect all players to treat each other with respect. Toxic language, harassment, hate speech, racism, and sexism will not be tolerated under any circumstances.

Penalty: Mute (1 hour to permanent depending on severity)

02. Hacking, Cheating & Modifications expand_more

Using hacked clients, cheats, macros, or modifications that grant players an unfair competitive advantage is strictly prohibited. Permitted mods include Optifine, Sodium, Minimap (without radar), and standard visual HUDs.

Penalty: Permanent IP Ban from the network

03. Exploits, Dupes & Glitches expand_more

Exploiting bugs, glitches, or duplication methods to spawn items or bypass game limits is forbidden. Please report bugs immediately using our Discord support desk or the support form here.

Penalty: Temp ban (7 days) & Full Inventory Reset

04. Store & Transaction Terms expand_more

Purchases on this store are mock integrations, but in actual play: chargebacks, scam disputes, or fraud lead to automatic blacklist bans. Delivery of items takes up to 5 minutes, requiring an online session.

Penalty: Blacklist from all affiliated Minecraft networks

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Find answers to billing, game mechanics, and account verification policies.

How long do purchases take to arrive? expand_more

Usually, store items arrive automatically in your inventory within 2 to 5 minutes of payment. Please make sure your Minecraft character is logged in to the ROXSMP server network when checkout processes.

What payment options are supported? expand_more

Through our simulated gateway payment, we accept a wide range of options including Credit/Debit Cards, PayPal, UPI, Netbanking, and popular Cryptocurrencies.

What should I do if my purchase did not arrive? expand_more

First, check that your transaction went through and wait up to 15 minutes. If items still haven't arrived, navigate to our Support tab, submit a Purchase Issue ticket with your transaction ID, and our administrator team will credit your items manually.

Are ranks permanent? expand_more

Yes, all VIP, VIP+, MVP, and Legendary ranks in this store are one-time lifetime purchases. Once purchased, they will remain on your account across server resets.
